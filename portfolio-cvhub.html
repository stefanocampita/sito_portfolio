CVHub — Business Project EcoNova

Published: Sep 22, 2025 • Reading time: ~3–4 min
Project type: Product & GTM Role: Solo author Tools: PowerPoint · Excel Slides: 12

Executive Summary

CVHub is a digital platform that turns static résumés into strategic tools. Users create a Master CV and generate role-specific versions in minutes. Target users are students, graduates and young professionals across Europe. Monetization combines a freemium B2C model (credit-based CV Wallet) with B2B institutional licenses.

Problem

  • Resumes are static and outdated
  • Job seekers struggle to stand out
  • Existing tools focus only on design

Solution

  • Master CV as central, updated profile
  • Role-specific CVs in minutes
  • Personal branding & strategy first

Business Model

  • Freemium B2C with credits (CV Wallet)
  • B2B licenses for universities
  • Scalable SaaS economics
