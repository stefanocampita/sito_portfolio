About me

I study how capital meets strategy — how businesses create value, how markets price it, and what drives decisions under uncertainty. My approach is rigorous and data-driven, with a strong focus on clarity and communication.

I like turning noisy financial information into structured analysis: valuation work, case studies, and weekly market recaps that connect fundamentals to real-world outcomes. This site is a curated collection of projects and briefs designed to show how I think, analyse, and explain.
